Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 05, 2026

Mathematics is the only backend we use. Xenz is designed with an "Offline-First" philosophy, meaning your financial data belongs strictly to your device.

1. Zero Data Collection

Xenz Expense Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial data on external servers. We have no access to your records, account balances, or spending habits.

2. Local Storage

All data you input into the app (Transactions, Categories, Budgets, Accounts) is stored locally on your device's internal storage using encrypted/secure SharedPreferences. This data remains on your phone and is never uploaded to a cloud or shared with third parties.

3. Security Features

The app provides a Passcode Lock feature. This is a local security measure to prevent unauthorized physical access to the app's UI. Your passcode is stored hash-locally and is never transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

Xenz is truly offline. It does not integrate with third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertisement networks that could potentially harvest your data. No internet permission is required to run the core features of the app.

5. Data Ownership & Deletion

You have absolute control over your data. If you choose to delete the application, all locally stored data is permanently wiped from your device. We do not maintain any backups or "shadow" copies of your records.
