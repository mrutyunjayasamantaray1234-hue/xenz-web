Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 05, 2026

1. Data Sovereignty

Xenz Expense Tracker operates on a strict "Local-First" principle. All data you input, including transactions, accounts, and categories, is stored exclusively on your device's local storage (SharedPreferences). We do not have servers, and we never collect, see, or transmit your financial data.

2. No Account Required

There is no login or account creation process. No email, phone number, or identity information is required or stored. Your identity remains completely anonymous to us.

3. Security

The app includes a built-in passcode lock feature to prevent unauthorized access on your device. Since we don't have access to your device, the security of your data depends on your device's physical security and the passcode you set within the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Xenz does not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We value your privacy as much as you do.
